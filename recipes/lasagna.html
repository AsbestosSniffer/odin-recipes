Easy Lasagna

Description

Easy lasagna recipe I copied from allrecipes.com.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 oz cottage cheese
  • 3 cups mozarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain ground beef, then stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Unconver and continue baking for 10 minutes.
