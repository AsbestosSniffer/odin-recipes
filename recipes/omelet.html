Minimalistic Omelet

Description

A very basic omelet. I make this when I'm in a hurry to get to a 9 am class at university.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 1 large tomato or 2 small ones
  • 1/4 of a medium-sized bell pepper
  • 4 large eggs
  • salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Dice the vegetables to a size of your liking. A food processor/chopper comes in handy here.
  2. Sauté the vegetables for 2 minutes on high heat. Then add the eggs.
  3. Sauté for about 2 more minutes. Add salt at the end.
